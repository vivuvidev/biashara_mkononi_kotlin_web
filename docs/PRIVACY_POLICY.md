# Privacy Policy — BiasharaMkononi POS

**Effective date:** [EFFECTIVE_DATE]

BiasharaMkononi POS ("the app", "we", "our") is a point-of-sale app for
small business owners. It helps you manage products, record sales,
print receipts, and track inventory directly on your phone or tablet.

This policy explains, in plain language, what information the app
handles and how it is used. We wrote it to be honest about exactly what
the app does today — nothing more.

## 1. What data the app collects

The app only collects the information **you** type into it while using
it as a business tool. This includes things like:

- Your shop name and basic shop details
- Product names, prices, quantities, and categories
- Sales records (items sold, amounts, dates, payment details you enter)
- Cashier or staff names you add to the app

We do not collect any data automatically beyond what you enter to run
your business in the app.

## 2. Where your data is stored

All of the data listed above is stored **locally on your own device**,
in the app's local database (Room/SQLite and local app storage). 

**There is currently no server or cloud account behind the app.**
Nothing you enter is uploaded, synced, or backed up to us or to any
third party. Your data stays on your device unless you choose to back
it up yourself (for example, through your phone's own backup features).

## 3. Who can access your data

Only you — the person using the app on your device — can access the
data stored in the app. We do not have accounts, servers, or any way to
view, copy, or retrieve your business data. We simply don't have it.

## 4. App permissions and why we ask for them

The app requests a small number of device permissions, each used only
for a specific feature:

| Permission | Why the app uses it |
|---|---|
| **Camera** | To scan product barcodes when adding or finding products. The camera is only active when you open the barcode scanner. |
| **Bluetooth** | To connect to and print receipts on Bluetooth receipt printers. Used only when you choose to print a receipt. |
| **Internet / Network state** | Reserved for future features (such as optional cloud backup or sync). At this time, the app does **not** use the internet to send, upload, or share any of your data. |

We do not use any of these permissions to collect, transmit, or share
your information.

## 5. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or transfer your data to anyone — there is
no third party for your data to go to, because the app does not send
data anywhere. There are no advertising networks, data brokers, or
partner integrations connected to the app.

## 6. Advertising, analytics, and tracking

The app contains **no advertising SDKs, no analytics tools, and no
tracking technologies**. We do not build profiles of you or your
business, and we do not monitor how you use the app.

## 7. Deleting your data

Because all data lives only on your device, you are always in full
control of it. You can:

- Delete individual products, sales, or records inside the app, or
- Uninstall the app, which permanently removes all of its locally
  stored data from your device.

We have no copy of your data to delete on our end, since none is ever
sent to us.

## 8. Your rights and ownership

You own and control all the business data you enter into the app —
your products, sales, and records belong to you. The app is simply a
tool to help you manage that information on your own device.

## 9. Children's privacy

BiasharaMkononi POS is a business tool intended for adult small
business owners and their staff. It is not designed for, marketed to,
or directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect data from
children.

## 10. Languages

The app is available in English and Swahili. This privacy policy may
be provided in either language; in case of any difference, the English
version applies.

## 11. Changes to this policy

If the app's features change in the future (for example, if we
introduce optional cloud backup or sync), this policy will be updated
to clearly describe what changes and what new data, if any, would be
involved — and we will make any new data use clear before it happens.
We will update the "Effective date" above whenever this policy changes.
